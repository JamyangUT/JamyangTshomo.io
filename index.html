Projects

Integrative Bioinformatics and ML Strategy for Identifying Lung Cancer Biomarkers
I performed differential gene expression (DEGs) analysis on eight datasets using GEO2R. From thousands of genes, significant DEGs were identified based on logFC and p-values. By comparing across datasets, I identified common genes for downstream analysis.
To explore gene interactions, I constructed protein–protein interaction (PPI) networks and identified key hub genes. These hub genes were then further analyzed and evaluated using machine learning models for their potential as biomarkers in lung cancer.
RNA-seq Data Analysis of Keratoconus and Control Myopia Patients
I performed RNA-seq data analysis on keratoconus and control myopia patient samples using the Galaxy Web Server. Raw files were processed through FastQC for quality control, followed by HISAT2 for alignment to the reference genome. Differential expression analysis was then carried out with EdgeR, identifying significant genes potentially involved in the pathogenesis of keratoconus.
I also conducted a literature review to contextualize the findings and connect results to current knowledge in the field.
Biological Database for Hutchinson Gilford Progeria Syndrome (HGPS) using OracleDB
I constructed Entity-Relationship (ER) diagrams for designing a biological database focused on HGPS. Extensive research and data retrieval were carried out to collect relevant biological information, which was then organized into attributes and relationships for accurate data modeling. Based on this design, I created a query-enabled database in OracleDB, capable of efficiently storing and retrieving biological data.
Alcohol Detecting System using Arduino Uno
I designed a real-time alcohol detection system using Arduino UNO as the microcontroller. The system was equipped with an MQ-3 gas sensor to detect alcohol vapors in the breath and trigger a response when levels crossed a threshold. I also integrated buzzer and LED indicators to provide immediate alerts.
